Vegetarian Chickpea Tacos

Description

A savory and delicious quick meal.

These tacos are jammed pack with chickpeas, taco seasoning and fresh toppings the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 (15 ounce) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, chopped

Directions

  1. Place chickpeas in a saucepan over medium heat. Add water and taco seasoning.
  2. Cook and stir until thoroughly heated, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from heat. Mash mixture to crush chickpeas
  3. Divide chickpea filling evenly among taco shells.
  4. add freshly chopped onions, tomato, and cilantro on top of chickpea filling

Enjoy!
